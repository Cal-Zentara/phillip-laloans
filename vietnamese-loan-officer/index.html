When you're processing all of that in a second language, you miss things — or you feel like you have to just trust and sign without fully understanding what you're agreeing to. That's not how I work.

I'll explain every document, every number, and every decision in the language you're most comfortable in. Whether that's fully in Vietnamese, a mix, or mostly English with key terms translated — I follow your lead.

Loan Programs

Every Loan Type, Explained Clearly

I'm backed by Loan Factory's network of 226+ lending partners — which means I can find the best rate and program for your specific situation, not just whatever one bank is offering today. Here's what's available:

FHA Loans
The most common starting point for first-time buyers. Lower down payment (as little as 3.5%), flexible credit requirements. I'll explain exactly what mortgage insurance means for your monthly payment.
Conventional Loans
For buyers with stronger credit and a down payment ready. Competitive rates and no lifetime mortgage insurance once you hit 20% equity.
CA Dream For All
California state program covering up to 20% of your down payment for qualifying first-time buyers. Many Vietnamese buyers in Orange County qualify — call me and I'll tell you in five minutes if you do.
VA Loans
Zero down, no PMI for veterans and active military. If you served, this is one of the best mortgage benefits available — and I'll make sure you use it correctly.
Jumbo Loans
For homes above conforming loan limits — common in Irvine and Newport Beach. I'll structure the right financing for higher-value purchases.
