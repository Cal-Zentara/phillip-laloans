The Orange Market

Buying a Home in Orange

The City of Orange has a character that sets it apart from the rest of OC. The historic Old Towne Orange district — with its antique shops, tree-lined streets, and early 20th-century homes surrounding the famous Orange Circle — draws buyers looking for something with more personality than a master-planned community. That character comes at a price, but Orange still offers relative value compared to coastal cities.

Orange is also a strong refinance market. If you bought in Orange a few years ago and haven't reviewed your rate, it's worth a conversation to see whether a refinance lowers your payment or shortens your payoff timeline. I'll run the numbers honestly — no pressure.
